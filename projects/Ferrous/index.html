Ferrous

A Unity 3D Adventure Puzzle Game

Ferrous is a student program at University of Toronto. By creating a single-player game, our goal is to build an attractive adventure game.

This was a 6 person team project where we created an ocean pollution-related game for the 2023 Tencent Youth Game Design Challenge.

Project Info:
  • Platform: Windows & Mac
  • Tools: Unity & C# & Maya
  • Date of Completion: 2023.12
  • Controller: Controller; Mouse & Keyboard
  • Art Style: Cartoon & realistic
  • Contribution: Game Programmer&Game Designer
  • Project Download Link: Ferrous-itch.io

What are we doing

Summary

Ferrous is an extraordinary adventure puzzle game that immerses players in a world where magnetism reigns supreme. With its captivating gameplay mechanics, intricately designed levels, and an array of challenges that evolve in complexity, it promises an unforgettable journey that hinges on the creative use of magnetic abilities in an ever-expanding and visually stunning world. Get ready to unleash your magnetic potential and embark on a thrilling robotic adventure like no other.

Key Feature

A key feature of the game lies in the ingenious ways players can employ their magnetic prowess to interact with the environment. In this magnetic adventure, players can effortlessly attract (pull) or repel (push) these objects to surmount obstacles, explore the surroundings, and advance through a multitude of meticulously designed levels.

 

Things I did

  1. Design of game levels
  2. Interactions between the player and level
  3. Game UI design and implement
  4. Audio Mixer in Unity

What I learned

  1. The development of different modules will be split and the way of aligning the functions will be specified to minimise problems when merging the code
  2. Design detail hints to guide players to utilise skills rather than confuse them
  3. Player feedback and playtests are very important to point out the shortcomings of the game and help improve it
  4. The use of the task planning system Trello facilitates the development process to a great extent
