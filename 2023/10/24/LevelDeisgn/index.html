Assignment 1 of Level Design Course

Intro

Hi, this is my first attempt to make a level design when I participated the course "Introduction to level Design".

The task is to create a level as reference to "Prison" in "Dishonored" as the pilot level. Using the Unreal Engine with "levelup tookit" developed by my schoolmates, I was able to produce this level quickly.

Design Sketch

I took a reference of five col of buildings, the escape plan is as the right graph.

Start from the first building, the player will learn the function of light, because they will enounter and die immediately once they hit the ligth. So I place the one static light at right and another moving at left. Player will also learn from the usage the door button and wire when the escape from the first room. They then will apply this discovery when they can't find the button of front door. They will realize the wire leading to another room, where it hides the button.

After escaping from the frist building. They will take a look of center and see the final destination. However they cannot get the destination but lead to another path to get out. They will entry the second area. where they will tranverse a complex structure of pipes and avoid light again. Here player will learn how to jump and climp the pipe boxes. They can see the path leading to second building out of window.

After they entry the third building, they will see a pole and pole hangers. Here they will learn how to use hangers to convoy to the destination.

The area 4 actually is the timing. The key to master this level is about the right time to jump. The end of hanger sometimes will leading to the abyss, so jump before it reach to the abyss.

After arrived at the area 5, they will realize they come back to the inner of the building . Where they can see where they came from and where to go. I previouly designed a global scanner vertically however it is not doable for this toolkit that I gave up to try. But the level is just about jump and apply what they previously learnt.
